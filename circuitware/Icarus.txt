Icarus Verilog help at:
https://steveicarus.github.io/iverilog/

To compile in current directory (applies to test benches also):
iverilog -o [executable name] [filename].v [otherfiles?].v 

To run:
vvp [executable name]

testing main structure:
iverilog -g2012 -o complilation_test synth.sv wave_period_calculator.sv i2s_transmitter.sv debouncer.sv

wave_period_calculator:
iverilog -g2012 -o tb_wave_period_calculator tb_wave_period_calculator.sv wave_period_calculator.sv
